# ASCENT — parking lot (good ideas deferred, not rejected)

Ideas we like but are intentionally NOT designing/tuning yet. Revisit later.

## Anchor weight scaling (parked 2026-05-31)
The Anchor could get **heavier as they collect boons / gain altitude**, so late-game Anchors
become nearly unthrowable tanks but increasingly terrain-problematic (sink weight-sensitive
ground faster, harder to fling across gaps, need more crew coordination to move).
- Pro: emergent late-game texture; "the prize gets heavier"; natural escalation of the
  carry/throw interdependence; a built-in soft difficulty curve.
- Why parked: we're stabilizing core tuning first (fixed Anchor mass). Layer scaling on once
  base grab/throw/carry feel is proven.
- Related: Bulwark "strength" stat could reduce encumbrance penalty → the natural Anchor-hauler.

## Bulwark strength → reduced carry penalty (parked 2026-05-31)
A strength/mass stat that lessens the grab-slow penalty, making specific roles better haulers.
Adds role texture to who carries the Anchor/heavy objects. Park until encumbrance baseline is tuned.
